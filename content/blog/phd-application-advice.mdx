When I was preparing my own PhD applications, I was fortunate to receive guidance from people who generously shared their time and experience during that stressful period. I've always tried to keep that habit of giving back — and this year, I'd like to continue doing so.

I'll be offering personalized feedback and advice to a group of applicants applying mainly to PhD programs in Computer Science or related fields.

I can help with:

- **Application materials** (SoP, Research Statement, CV)
- **Defining research focus** and choosing programs
- **Overall application strategy** and preparation

Since my time is limited, I won't be able to respond to everyone individually. I'll select a group of applicants based on their level of preparation and alignment with my expertise, so I can offer thoughtful and useful guidance.

If you're applying this year and would like to be considered, please fill out [this short form](https://lnkd.in/eQxkEEbu).

Wishing everyone a smooth and focused application season ahead.
