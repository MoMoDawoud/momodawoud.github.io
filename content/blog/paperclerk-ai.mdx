Monday: "This paper looks essential!" *download*

Tuesday: "Ooh, looks interesting!" *download*

Wednesday: "Just in case..." *download*

Thursday: "Wait, didn't I already...?"

Friday: 500 PDFs stare back, unread.

If this feels a little too familiar... same.

That's why I built **PaperClerk AI** — a lightweight, local, AI-powered assistant that finally keeps your research library under control.

## What it does

- **Finds all your PDFs** scattered across your machine
- **Summarizes them** with a local LLaMA model (via [Ollama](https://ollama.com/))
- **Tells you exactly what the paper is about** — no more guessing from filenames
- **Lets you auto-keep / auto-skip / auto-archive** based on your interests
- **Emails you a neat digest** every day or week

All private. All local. All fast.

If your "Read Later" folder has quietly become a PDF graveyard... this might actually help.

Check it out on [GitHub](https://github.com/MoMoDawoud/PaperClerk).
